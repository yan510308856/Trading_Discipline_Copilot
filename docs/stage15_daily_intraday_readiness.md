# Stage 15 - Daily Intraday Readiness

## Why daily readiness exists

Intraday trading needs preparation before the first trade idea appears. The daily readiness checklist is a discipline gate that asks whether the trader has prepared the day, checked risks, and defined limits before planning intraday trades.

## Required items

- Select today's watchlist before the open.
- Assess today's market environment.
- Check important events and scheduled risks.
- Review existing swing positions.
- Define today's risk limits.

All required items must be complete before the app marks the day as cleared for intraday trading.

## Optional items

- Confirm platform, data, and broker access are working.
- Check mental and physical state.
- Confirm there is no need to force a trade today.

Optional items support discipline, but they do not block readiness status in Stage 15.

## What cleared means

`Cleared` means the preparation checklist is complete enough to start planning intraday trades. It does not mean any trade is valid. The normal pre-trade rules, blockers, warnings, and risk checks still apply.

## What remains manual

Stage 15 keeps these tasks manual:

- Economic and scheduled event checks.
- Watchlist selection.
- Market environment assessment.
- Swing position review.
- Daily risk limit definition.

## Deferred

- Automatic economic calendar integration.
- Broker integration.
- Automatic watchlist generation.
- Automatic market environment classification.
- Strict trade-horizon database gating.
- Any order placement, modification, cancellation, or auto-trading.
